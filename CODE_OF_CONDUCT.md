# Code Of Conduct

We want `zt-ui` to be a calm, rigorous, and welcoming project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in critique and generous in interpretation.
- Focus on the work, not the person.
- Explain technical disagreement with evidence and context.
- Make space for newer contributors to ask basic questions.
- Prefer clarity, patience, and precision over status signaling.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive or hostile behavior toward contributors.
- Repeated bad-faith arguing or deliberate derailment.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Maintainer Responsibility

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject content that makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive. Repeated violations can lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from the project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

- use a private moderation or support channel if the repository host provides one,
- or open a minimal public issue requesting moderator contact without sharing sensitive details.
